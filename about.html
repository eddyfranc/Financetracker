About HarakaPay Freelance

HarakaPay is designed to make freelancing simple and stress-free by offering fast and secure payment options.

HarakaPay is built for freelancers across Africa and beyond. We provide a unified platform that integrates multiple payment methods like M-Pesa, PayPal, and others. With our easy-to-use invoicing tool, you can generate invoices, track payments, and withdraw funds instantly.

Our mission is to help freelancers focus on what they do best creating great work without worrying about delayed payments or complicated billing processes.
